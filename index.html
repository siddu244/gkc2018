Go karting championship 2018 is a national level go kart designing, manufacturing and racing event for real motorsports enthusiasts. The event is formulated to empower engineering students the skill of designing go kart racing vehicle, building it on their own and race it on country’s finest circuit under FMSCI in Garden city Bengaluru. It’s all about how well you make your dream of Building your own racing car come true. The racing festival provides students a platform to take off in flying colors. Build your portfolio strong by taking part in the championship, also win up to Rupees 3.6 lakh worth prizes, placements, goodies and lot more. Go Kart Championship 2018, ideal platform for students to learn the basics of Automobiles, Vehicle dynamics then design, fabricate, optimize and race their own racing machines.
